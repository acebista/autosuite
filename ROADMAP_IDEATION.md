# Tech-Savvy Dealer Roadmap
**Vision:** Move from "Data Management" to "Agentic Automation".

## 1. Omnichannel Communications (The "Killer App")
*   **Problem:** Dealers still manually switch between the CRM and WhatsApp on their phones.
*   **Solution:** Deep integration.
    *   **Click-to-Chat:** (Implemented ✅) Open WhatsApp Web/App directly from the lead profile.
    *   **Template Library:** "1-Click Send" for Brochures, Test Drive Maps, and Price Lists.
    *   **Automated Triggers:** System sends a "Thanks for visiting" WhatsApp message 1 hour after a showroom visit automatically.

## 2. Transparent Service Experience
*   **Problem:** Customers feel anxious about their car in the shop and call frequently.
*   **Solution:** "Uber-style" tracking.
    *   **Live Job Link:** SMS a link to the customer where they can see stages: "In Bay" -> "Washing" -> "Ready".
    *   **Digital Approval:** Technician uploads a photo of a rusty part. Customer clicks "Approve Repair" (₹5,000) on their phone. No phone calls needed.

## 3. Gamified Sales Performance
*   **Problem:** Sales reps are competitive but lack real-time visibility into their earnings.
*   **Solution:** A "Money Dashboard".
    *   **Live Commission:** "You made ₹12,000 today."
    *   **Target Tracker:** "Sell 1 more car to hit Gold Tier."
    *   **Leaderboard:** TV Mode for the showroom floor showing top performers.

## 4. Digital Document Workflow
*   **Problem:** Printing contracts, signing them, and scanning them back is wasteful.
*   **Solution:** Tablet-first workflow.
    *   **e-Signatures:** Customer signs on the iPad.
    *   **Cloud Vault:** Documents are auto-saved to the customer's secure folder.
    *   **Email Copy:** Customer gets the signed PDF instantly.

## 5. AI Sales Assistant
*   **Problem:** Reps waste time on "cold" leads.
*   **Solution:** Predictive AI.
    *   **"Who to Call":** The app generates a daily playlist of the 10 most likely buyers.
    *   **Sentiment Analysis:** "This customer sounds angry in the notes, flag for Manager."
